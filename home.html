CHARLES BUKOWSKI
The quintessential counterculture icon.

A hard-drinking wild man of literature and a stubborn outsider to the poetry world, he wrote unflinchingly about booze, women and work, in raw street-tough poems whose truth has struck a chord wih generations of readers. Charles Bukowski is one of America's best-known contemporary writers and its most influential and imitated poet.

Just a taste of the profoundness that is Bukowski..
